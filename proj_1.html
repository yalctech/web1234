How to Make A Chocolate Dome

Ingredients

  • 1 cup melted dark chocolate
  • 1 brownie
  • Assorted berries for decoration
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1/3 cup caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Cover blown up balloon with melted dark chocolate 2/3 way up.
  2. Leave to cool down for 20 minutes or until fully hardens.
  3. Place brownie on a serving plate, add berries and a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  4. Pop the balloon to release the chocolate sphere.
  5. Cover the brownie with chocolate sphere and pour heated caramel over until it melts.
